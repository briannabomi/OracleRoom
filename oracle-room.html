Traffic Light System

One traffic light. That's the entire safety system — kept deliberately simple, so you never have to think about it under pressure. Call your color out any time.

Green

Everything's clear. Keep going.

Yellow

Pause. I want to keep going — I just need to slow down, change the intensity, or talk something through first.

Red

Stop. Everything, right now. No reason needed, and we don't discuss it — for any reason. Red is your safe word.

Blue

Checked out — anywhere from bored to fully gone, “I've left my body.”

You can be more than one at once — yellow and blue, or red and blue. Name both, and I'll meet you where you actually are.
